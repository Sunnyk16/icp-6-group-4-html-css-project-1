Jalebi

Jalebi is a popular sweet snack in South Asia, Middle East, Africa, and Mauritius. It is made of deep-fried batter that is then soaked in sugar syrup. The dish is often served during festivals and special occasions

Kaju katli

Kaju katli, also known as kaju barfi, is a popular Indian sweet made with cashew nuts, sugar, and cardamom powder. It is characterized by its diamond shape and smooth, melt-in-your-mouth texture

Rasmalai

Rasmalai is a Classic Indian dessert consisting of juicy cheese discs served with thickened milk.These sweet, juicy and soft cheesy morsels are addictive and super delicious.
